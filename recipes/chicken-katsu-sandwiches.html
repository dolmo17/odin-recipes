Chicken Katsu Sandwiches

Description

Panko-breaded, pan-fried, katsu-style chicken cutlets topped with cool shredded lettuce and a mayo-pickle hot sauce!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/4 cup chopped pickles
  • 2 Tbsp. plus 1/4 cup hot sauce
  • 1 tsp. kosher salt (more as needed)
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 3 Tbsp. cayenne pepper
  • 2 tsp. garlic powder
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 cups panko breadcrumbs
  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken thighs, lightly pounded to 1/2" thick
  • 1/4 cup (or more) extra-virgin olive oil
  • 8 slices of bread
  • Shredded iceberg lettuce for serving
